\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Provides graphical or textual output for film analysis results. 

The \doxylink{class_result_displayer}{Result\+Displayer} is responsible for visualizing the data collected in the \doxylink{class_film_statistics}{Film\+Statistics} object. This includes summaries, distributions of shot types, timelines, or other outputs meant for the user.

This class currently stores a copy of the \doxylink{class_film_statistics}{Film\+Statistics} object. For performance or design flexibility, a reference-\/based approach may also be considered depending on use case.

Future implementations may include rendering to GUI, charts, or image overlays.
